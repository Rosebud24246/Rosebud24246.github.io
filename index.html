Simplicity with a purpose

What do I do?

I create graphics for websites, games, and other multimedia platforms. I specialize in creating monochrome graphics or doing small pixel art animations.

What tools am I profficient in?

I use Clip Studio Paint, FireAlpaca, Photoshop, and Aseprite when creating graphics and animation.

What makes me unique?

I like to emphasize simplicity and meaning in my works. I think the minimalist approach is effective and meaningful, thus, I dont use abundant color in my works unless necessary.
